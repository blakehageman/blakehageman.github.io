Context

Submitted in December 2022 for Buildner's MICROHOME 5 design competition. Winners not yet announced.

Competition brief: design "an off-grid modular structure to accomodate a hypothetical young professional couple. The only requirement is that the structure's total floor area [excludes outdoor spaces] does not exceed 25 m2."

Description

Garden House is a small, affordable, low-tech sanctuary that brings nature and beauty to a utility building typology. Designed for both standalone and infill sites, the structure uses readily-available materials and methods to create a high performing shell with bright and airy interiors.
