Ode to a Module

September 2021

May the eyes of the Architect see such that the world is recreated in her mind. Let her coordinate the efforts of many men with this image—a shared consciousness. Your purpose, o module, is to structure this consciousness and give it form for just use. Will you unify our benevolent efforts into a solitary force? ➔

The New American Dream

September 2020

The American Dream is founded on the concurrent pursuit of both purpose and property. Its prerequisite is a society of opportunity, and its modern iconography is the single-family home. What better symbol of the sovereign individual than a personal castle for you and your family, complete with a white picket fence at the boundary of your domain? ➔

The City in Time

June 2018

In bustling areas of activity, does time accelerate with the increased tempo of comings and goings, pleases and thank yous, hellos and goodbyes? Perhaps the quiet neighborhoods slow down just so we can savor moments with our children before they grow up. Often somewhere in between the two, the city landscape is a patchwork of these fasts and slows. ➔
